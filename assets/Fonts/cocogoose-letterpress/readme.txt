Cocogoose Letterpress is a variant of our CocoGoose Typeface, that uses opentype substitutions randomly choose each letter between four variants, for a real letterpress look without digital - looking repeating letters.

This font is TOTALLY FREE for NONCOMMERCIAL USES.

For commercial uses you must get a license at:
http://www.zetafonts.com/cocogoose

The Cococogoose family features also Inline, Outlined, Regular and Light Versions. Download them for free on http://www.zetafonts.com 

Cocogoose Letterpress is part of the Coco Gothic superfamily:
http://www.zetafonts.com/coco-gothic

Have a question?: info@zetafonts.com

Please notice that random letter substitutions only work on open-type enabled software.
